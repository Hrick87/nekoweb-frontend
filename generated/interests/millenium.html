MILLENIUM

What is Millenium?

Millenium is a TTRPG focused on survival, weapon building, exploration, and monster hunting. The combat seeks to emulate the feeling of a movement shooter, rewarding characters for never staying in one spot for too long, and allows for things like wall running and knockback.

Features

  • Classless Character Creation
  • Easy to learn 2d10 rolls for everything
  • In-depth weapon customization
  • A spontaneous and modular "magic system"
  • A simple but intricate crafting system
  • A simple but robust influence system
  • Fauna and plant life in the environment matter
  • Unique combat system mimicking a movement shooter

How can I play?

As of now the game is in a private testing stage. After this has finished, a public testing phase will be announced and linked here. Stay tuned for the open beta release date!
